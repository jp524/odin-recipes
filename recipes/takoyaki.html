Takoyaki Recipe

Ingredients

Dough

  • 100g flour
  • 1tsp (5g) dashi powder
  • 400ml water
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup cold butter

Fillings

  • Octopus
  • Potato chips
  • Red pickled ginger
  • Green onion

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Steps

  1. Mix dough ingredients and prepare the sauce.
  2. Place dough into takoyaki pan and add fillings.
  3. Once the takoyaki are done, top with sauce.
